before doing exercises...

  • Check all your exercise equipments if those are functional. Playing your favorite music during exercise's keeps you motivated!
  • Whatever your goals and medical condition, approach any new exercise regimen with caution.
  • After the warm up you should stretch your muscles and hold it for about 15 seconds. When the muscles are warmed up you are less likely to get injured.
  • Do some less strenuous exercises to cool your body down after the more intense part of the workout.
